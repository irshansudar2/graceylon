The Legacy of Love

Where Passion Meets Heritage

Derived from the Gaelic word grá for love and the historic, fertile lands of Ceylon, our brand is a passionate tribute to Sri Lanka’s unparalleled natural treasures.

Every single leaf and bark quill is hand-harvested by local multi-generational farming communities, ensuring the preservation of both ancient farming traditions and the surrounding delicate ecosystems.

Artisanal Harvesting

Only the absolute top two leaves and a tender bud are hand-picked at precise morning dew hours.

Ethical Foundations

Reinvesting 15% of all proceeds directly back into the education and healthcare of Sri Lankan estate communities.
